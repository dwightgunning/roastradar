About

Roast Radar aims to be a quick and easy way to locate specialty coffee roasters around the world.

Why? I love to try out speciality coffee from local roasters whenever I'm travelling. I've found they're not always easy to identify via quick searches on Google Maps, usually made in a hurry from my mobile.

So the idea here is to give quick and easy access to a curated set of roasters for easy reference, any time and any place.

Contributions

All suggestions and contributions are welcome.

If you know of a great specialty roaster that's missing or there's an error on the site, let me know via email.

The website code is open source. Check out the project and feel free to submit issues or pull requests.
